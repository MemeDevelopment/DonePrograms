1 - Head over to LoggingIn.Java
2 - Remove "INSERT_BOT_TOKEN" and insert your discord bots token from https://discordapp.com/developers/applications/me
3 - Head over to MessageReceived and change "INSERT_BOT_ID" with the id of your bot.
4 - In MessageReceived add the github repo link for your bot if you like or keep blank.
5 - To add the bot to your server, you need admin on the server you would like to add too and
change INSERT_BOT_ID in the link to the bot's id, then proceed to click the link. 
https://discordapp.com/oauth2/authorize?&client_id=INSERT_BOT_ID&scope=bot
